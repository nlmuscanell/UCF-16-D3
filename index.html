Sociodemographic Factors & Health in the U.S.



Correlations: Poverty, Obesity, Healthcare, and Smoking

The data presented is in the scatterplot is based on sociodemographic and health data from the US Census Bureau (2014 estimates). The data set includes data on rates of income, obesity, poverty, etc. by state. This scatterplot shows the relationships between poverty and healthcare status, and obesity and smoking for each state in the U.S. Visually, it appears that there are small to moderate positive relationships between the x-axis variables (poverty, healthcare) and the y-axis variables (obesity, smoking).
